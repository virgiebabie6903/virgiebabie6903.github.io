Portfolio

Rivals of Aether Workshop

Pixel artist, animator, coder, designer, etc.

(Jan. 2021 - Present)

DISCLAIMER: I am NOT affiliated with Aether Studios or Dan Fornace, the creators of Rivals of Aether. In my spare time, I work on player-created characters, stages, and other content supported by the game's workshop functionality. In this gallery, you'll be able to see some workshop creations that I either created by myself or heavily contributed to.

Gabe Gator in: Gator Crossing

Pixel artist, animator, coder, designer, etc.

(Feb. 2024 - Apr. 2024)

A solo project done for a game art class at UCF. Though the main focus was on the art, animation and concept art, I also used my knowledge of coding in Game Maker Studio 2 to create a quick and dirty but satisfying to control platformer in the process.

Chrono Gear: Warden of Time

Pixel artist, pixel animator, play tester

(Sept. 2024 - Mar. 2025)

A Hololive fan-game being worked on by Sabrina DiDuro, the creator of the Freedom Planet series. I primarily worked on animating smaller NPCs and enemies, though I also had experience play-testing and bug-reporting in my time on the project.

Queso Caper!

Creative director, 2D/3D Character/Prop artist

(May. 2025 - Jun. 2025)

A team project worked on for my game production class at UCF in 2025. I served as the creative director, guiding the overall visual style of the game, as well as providing almost all 2D assets as well as the player, enemy and collectible models and animations.

Stunt Triple: Hollywood Hero

Character designer, Concept artist, Mocap director

(May. 2025 - Jun. 2025)

Another team project for my game production class at UCF. I was the primary character designer, working on 2D designs for another team member to adapt into 3D. I also provided art for the game's menus and some props. In addition, I worked with motion capture for the first time, serving as the choreography director for our animation session.
